## Entity Translation Pattern

For the long-form `src/data` entities, translate these fields while preserving each entity key and slug:

- `name`: translate only when the name is a generic activity, e.g. `Helicopter Tour` → `Tour en hélicoptère`; preserve proper place names.
- `description`: translate fully.
- `tagline`: translate fully.
- `description[]`: translate each paragraph fully.
- `tips[]`: translate each tip fully.
- `highlights[]`, `included[]`, `bring[]`: translate each item fully.
- `info.*` display values: translate human-readable labels and values, preserve coordinates, numbers, prices, URLs, and machine keys.

Example:

| Field | English | French |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `entities.activity.swim-with-dolphins.name` | Swim with Dolphins | Nager avec les dauphins |
| `entities.activity.swim-with-dolphins.description` | Experience the magic of swimming alongside wild dolphins in their natural habitat on the west coast of Mauritius. | Vivez la magie de nager aux côtés de dauphins sauvages dans leur habitat naturel, sur la côte ouest de Maurice. |
| `entities.beach.trou-aux-biches.name` | Trou aux Biches | Trou aux Biches |
| `entities.beach.trou-aux-biches.description` | Paradise with white sand, crystal clear azure water and coconut trees. Perfect for swimming, scuba diving and windsurfing. | Un paradis de sable blanc, d'eau azur cristalline et de cocotiers. Parfait pour la baignade, la plongée sous-marine et la planche à voile. |
